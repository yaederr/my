Mack Road Tractors

An American Vehicle Company with a Long History

Scott Truck Line Mack

Timeline of Mack Road Tractors:

  • 1900 - First vehicle produced
  • 1911 - Merger with Saurer Motor Truck Company
  • 1920 - First truck in the market equipped with power brakes
  • 1922 - Bulldog mascot is adopted and is still in use today
  • 1932 - Bulldog hood ornament is adopted and is still in use today
  • 1956 - Mack acquires Brockway and closes down the brand
  • 1966 - Assembly plant in Oakville, Ontario, Canada is opened
  • 1970 - New assembly plant in Allentown
  • 1990 - The company is owned completely by Renault
